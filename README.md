# 🌿 Terra Tracker — Carbon Footprint Calculator

**Terra Tracker** is an interactive, single-page web application designed for **The Nature Conservancy**. It empowers individuals to calculate, visualize, and reduce their annual carbon footprint through a science-backed, user-friendly interface.

---

## ✨ Features

* **Multi-Step Calculator**: A 4-part assessment covering Travel, Home Energy, Food & Diet, and Lifestyle.
* **Dynamic Results Dashboard**: Visualizes emissions in tonnes of $CO_2e$ per year using interactive progress bars and donut charts.
* **Global Benchmarking**: Compares your results against averages from India ($1.9t$), the US ($14.2t$), and the World ($7.0t$).
* **Actionable Tips**: Provides a filterable library of over 15+ practical strategies to reduce environmental impact.
* **Educational Hub**: A "Learn" section featuring key climate facts, such as the $2t$ sustainable per-person target.

---

## 🛠️ Technical Stack

* **Language**: HTML5, CSS3, and Vanilla JavaScript.
* **Typography**: Uses *Playfair Display* for headings and *Nunito* for body text.
* **Styling**: Custom CSS variables and responsive Grid/Flexbox layouts.

## 📊 The Science

The calculator uses estimated emission factors to provide a baseline of your environmental impact:
* **Global Average**: The current global average is approximately **7 tonnes** per year.
* **Sustainable Target**: To meet Paris Agreement goals, the target is to stay under **2 tonnes** per person.
